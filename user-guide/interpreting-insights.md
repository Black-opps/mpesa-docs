# Interpreting Insights & Analytics 📈

Understand what the platform tells you about your finances.

---

## Overview Dashboard

| Metric                  | What it means                     |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **Total Transactions**  | Total number of M-PESA movements  |
| **Total Amount**        | Net money flow (Received - Sent)  |
| **Average Transaction** | Typical size of your transactions |
| **Active Days**         | Days you had financial activity   |

---

## Insights Section

The platform generates three types of insights:

### 1. Spending Patterns

- "Your average transaction is KES 2,450"
- "You send more money on Fridays"

### 2. Alerts

- Large transactions above your average
- Unusual spikes in spending
- Frequent small transfers (possible salary splitting)

### 3. Recommendations

- "Consider negotiating better rates with frequent recipients"
- "You have high transport costs this month"
- "Top 3 customers represent 68% of your revenue"

## How to Use Insights Effectively

1. Review **Top Customers** regularly
2. Watch for **large transactions** alerts
3. Use **transaction types** breakdown to understand your business
4. Compare months to spot seasonal patterns

---

**Pro Tip**: Upload statements consistently every month for the most accurate insights and trends.
